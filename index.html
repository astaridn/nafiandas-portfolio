    Kendaraan Listrik Langkah Kecil Berdampak Besar bagi Bumi

    VIK (Visual Interactive Kompas) is a new journalism concept from Kompas.com for entering the digital realm. VIK presents reports and information in multimedia formats (text, photos, audio, video, and infographics).

    In collaboration with PLN, me and my colleagues created a VIK “Kendaraan Listrik Langkah Kecil Berdampak Besar bagi Bumi”. It contains information about the benefits and positive impacts of using electric vehicles, both personally and environmentally.

    UI/UX Case Study:
    Yearly Reading Wrap Up on Goodreads

    Closing the learning activities at Dibimbing.id as a UI/UX Research and Design student, I completed a Final Project with the Case Study. This final project is made based on user experience using Goodreads. Goodreads is an app aims to help users find new books, track their reading progress, and engage with a community of fellow readers.

    To improve user experience, i conducted UI and UX research for the app. The main feedback from the user are: unappealing user interface and lack of personalization. From the research, an idea is to create a new features to the Goodreads Mobile app that includes a summary and review of user book consumption over a year.

    Visual Interactive Kompas:
    Merapah Trans-Jawa 2024

    Merapah is signature program from Kompas.com that takes the form of a journey, exploring the progress of infrastructure development and accessibility, the state of public facilities, and the tourist spots and local culture along the travel route.

    In 2024, Merapah Trans-Jawa expedition collaborated with PT Astra Daihatsu Motor as the main sponsor, supported by Eiger, Sompo Insurance, and the Directorate General of Highways from the Ministry of Public Works and Housing (PUPR). Merapah Trans-Jawa 2024 will follow the Central and Southern Java routes, from Jakarta to Banyuwangi, East Java. This journey will be presented in an exclusive, multi-platform format, including a special VIK (Visual Interactive Kompas).

    JEO: Sebaran Jalan Rusak

    JEO is one of the channels on Kompas.com that features in-depth news coverage. The goal of this channel is to provide deeper insights into events. This means JEO aims to clarify the facts amidst the vast amount of information circulating in the public.

    In the JEO Bina Marga article titled "Banyak Jalan Rusak di Daerah, Pemerintah Pusat Pun Turun Tangan" ("Many Damaged Roads in the Regions, the Central Government Intervenes"), I developed an infographic titled "Sebaran Jalan Rusak" ("Distribution of Damaged Roads"), which details the state of roads in Indonesia along with the relevant road condition standards. Additionally, I designed the menu buttons and the cover for the JEO page.
